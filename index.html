Why most outreach fails

Not because the offer is bad — because the outreach lacks structure. Bad targeting, poor framing, and no usage rules destroy deliverability and reputation. Inbox Ignition fixes the foundation first.

What the Inbox Ignition Pack includes

1. Strategic Foundation

We map your offer, ICP, pricing, and primary goal before writing a single email. This ensures the outreach feels intentional and avoids wasted sends.

Deliverables:
  • Offer clarity brief
  • ICP & target filter
  • Positioning notes for messages

2. 5-Step High-Intent Sequence

A 5-email flow engineered to start a conversation — curiosity, credibility, pain, value, breakup. Each email has two subject options and natural, human copy.

Includes:
  • 2 subject-line variants per email
  • Personalization & send instructions
  • Follow-up cadence

3. Psychology-Driven Copy

Trust-first framing, subtle persuasion, and emotional triggers tuned to your niche. The copy is manual, bespoke, and never templated-for-everyone.

4. Clear Usage Instructions

Clear rules: when to send, daily caps, personalization shortcuts, and what damages deliverability.

5. Feedback & Optimization

Share performance, request refinements, and turn the pack into a living asset that improves with data.

More replies. More qualified conversations. More booked calls — without sounding needy or robotic.
